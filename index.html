Why Learn from Paola?

I'm not your typical English teacher. I spent 10 years teaching English to professionals — from designing training programs at the UN Human Rights Office in Colombia to serving as an IELTS and Cambridge oral examiner.

But then I changed careers. I studied Information Engineering in Germany, worked as a Software QA tester, and completed a thesis on machine learning for grid load prediction.

Now I combine both worlds: I understand the technical challenges you face because I've lived them. I've written code, reviewed pull requests, sat in standups, and struggled to explain complex systems to non-technical stakeholders — all in English.

That's what makes this different: You're learning from someone who speaks both languages — English and Tech.

Asking for Clarification Without Sounding Incompetent

Get the information you need without seeming confused. Frame ambiguity as a problem to solve, not a gap in your understanding.
